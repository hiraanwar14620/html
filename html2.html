Introduction

Pakistan Urdu: پَاکِسْتَان officially the Islamic Republic of Pakistan (اِسْلامی جَمْہُورِیَہ پَاکِسْتَان), is a country in South Asia. It is the world's fifth-most populous country,
with a population of almost 243 million people, and has the world's second-largest Muslim population

The Provinces and administrative territories of Pakistan are subdivided into administrative "divisions", Divisional Commissioner is the administrative head of a division.
Divisional Commissioner is appointed by the government of Pakistan from Pakistan Administrative Service
